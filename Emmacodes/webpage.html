Maverick City Music

ABOUT

Maverick City Music is an American contemporary worship Music group
collectively founded by Tony Brown and Jonathan Jay originating
from Atlanta. They are a gospel singing Music group, preaching
the gospel of Christ through their inspiring songs. They have inspired
several lives all over the world by their inspirational gospel songs.
The mucic style of maverick city is unique. Their songs have given hope
to many lives and courage to many others, passing through dark times.
Songs by Maverick City Music are often times interesting, inspirational
and pleasant. As a renowned gospel singing group, their songs have spread
far and wide, and are being listened to by christians and believers
over the world.

Maverick city music have been known globally for their various works
of gospel music. They consist of many people who have also been saved
by the grace of God. Their faithful work in the music ministry cannot
be forgotten because they have served faithfully in the vineyard of God.
Some of their hit songs are; Jireh, Promises, Million Little Miracles,
Firm Foundation, amongst others.
